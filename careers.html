Careers at SVALLY

SVALLY does not hire impulsively. Roles are opened only when systems require reinforcement. This page exists to clarify expectations before any conversation begins.

Who We Work With

SVALLY collaborates with individuals who value structure, clarity, and long-term thinking over chaos-driven creativity.

  • Comfortable with documentation and standards
  • Capable of independent execution
  • Open to feedback and iteration
  • Focused on quality under deadlines

Video Editor / Producer

Responsible for executing content according to defined templates, pacing standards, and platform requirements.

  • Short-form and long-form editing
  • Retention-focused cuts
  • Template-based workflows
  • Collaboration with QA reviewers

Quality Reviewer (QA)

Responsible for reviewing assets before delivery, ensuring technical, narrative, and brand consistency.

  • Attention to detail
  • Clear written feedback
  • Understanding of platform specs
  • Process-oriented mindset

Open Applications

When no roles are listed, open applications may still be accepted. All submissions are reviewed asynchronously.

Only candidates aligned with SVALLY’s operating principles will receive a response.
